About Aluna

Aluna was born, like every design project, from a need — the need to grow, to reach further, and to make design accessible to everyone. What began as a creative vision has evolved into a studio that brings thoughtful, high-quality design to a wide range of Cuban businesses and brands. Today, Aluna has successfully expanded beyond borders, establishing its presence in the international graphic design market.

Working at Aluna

We’re looking for designers driven by big challenges and
meaningful experiences.

Every member of our team has the opportunity to work closely in a collaborative, hands-on environment, where creativity and problem-solving take center stage. Our projects span from supporting local Cuban businesses to collaborating with international brands, giving our designers the chance to grow, experiment, and leave a real impact. Guided by a design-led philosophy, we foster an open and inspiring culture where ideas are valued and innovation thrives. If you’re ready to push boundaries and be part of a team that’s redefining design across borders, we’d love to hear from you.
